About me

High-achieving Sports Analytics student and Chancellor's List honoree (4.0 GPA) at UNC Charlotte, with a decade as a business operator and hands-on experience in data analysis, predictive modeling, and customer analytics. I turn data into decisions — and build the tools that act on it.

I am a Sports Analytics major at UNC Charlotte with a deep-rooted passion for the statistics that drive the games we love. While my technical focus is on optimizing performance and strategy in sports, I am equally driven by a passion for digital privacy. I believe that as we collect more data — on the field and off — we have a growing responsibility to handle that information ethically.

My goal is to merge these interests, exploring how high-level analytics can coexist with robust data privacy standards. 10+ years of business experience applying analytics across fleet management, e-commerce, events, and residential services.
